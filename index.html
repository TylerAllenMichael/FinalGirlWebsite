About

Final Girl is a fan site about the popular horror franchise Scream. As a life long horror geek, the Scream movies have been some of my favourite films of all time. With this website, I hope to showcase my design skills and my love for the franchise as a whole.

What makes Scream so amazing is the fact that it has self-aware characters who understand common tropes and horror cliches from the original slashers. Best of luck, and I hope you will survive my site.
